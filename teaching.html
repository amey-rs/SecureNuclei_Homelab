LAB 1 : Security Homelab Setup

The objective of Lab 1 is to guide users through the process of setting up their homelab environment. By the end of this lab, users should have a functional homelab ready for further exploration and security operations.

Prerequisite: Virtual Machine and Linux ISO.

Knowledge Test: MCQ
